<h5 style="text-align: left;">'Insomnia: Hardcore' is a Vanilla+ modpack that is all about immersion as well as enhancing the experience of playing Hardcore with friends. The modpack also adds new content and mechanics without going overboard.</h5>
<pre style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</pre>
<pre style="text-align: left;"><br /><img src="https://i.imgur.com/zt8Kn0i.png" /><br /><br /></pre>
<pre style="text-align: left;"><img src="https://i.imgur.com/sCigkI6.png" /></pre>
<pre> <br /><br /></pre>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><strong>-[ Revive System ]-</strong></h3>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">With this modpack you don't respawn. Rather, when you get killed you enter a downed state in which your friends will be able to revive you. But it isn't as simple as just getting to the dead person and reviving them. You first have to find a Cleric Villager and buy the 'Revive Star' to do so.</h5>

</div>

<h3 style="text-align: left;"><strong>-[ Origins and Classes]-</strong></h3>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">When you first load into a world you get greeted by a choice to choose an Origin and a Class. Which can have several fun quirks as well various buffs and debuffs.</h5>

<h3 style="text-align: left;"><strong>-[ Beautiful World Generation ]-</strong></h3>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Using the 'Terralith 2.0' mod in combination with the 'Caves and Cliffs Part 2' updates already amazing worldgen gives some absolutely crazy sights. The Nether has also received a makeover with the 'Amplified Nether' mod.</h5>

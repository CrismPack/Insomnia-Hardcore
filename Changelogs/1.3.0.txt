v1.3.0 - Major Update
Disclaimer: This version is not compatible with worlds generated prior to the update.

Fabric version: Fabric-0.14.19
Update summary: 
~ Revamped title screen with better dynamic scaling of elements.
~ Now includes shaderspacks with a Quality and Performance configuration to choose from.
~ Caves now get completely dark and torches held by players emit light.
~ Added improved monster and animal animations.
~ Overhauls for a bunch of the vanilla buildings.
~ Reworked the respawning system to allow for players to get a second chance as well as getting absorption effect upon respawning.
~ Player spawn locations are now randomized on servers.
~ New combat system and additional weapons.
~ Added proximity voice chat functionality with realistic reverb.
~ Replaced home teleport commands with a recall potion.
~ Players now get a starter house when joining for the first time.

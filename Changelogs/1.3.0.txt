v1.3.0 - Major Update
Disclaimer: This version is not compatible with worlds generated prior to the update.

Fabric version: Fabric-0.14.18
Update summary: 
~ Revamped the title screen to have better dynamic scaling of elements.
~ Now includes shaders by default with a Quality and Performance configuration to choose from.
~ Caves now get completely dark.
~ Torches held by players now emit light.
~ Added improved monster and animal animations from 'FreshAnimations'.
~ New zombie textures and sounds from 'Tissou's Zombie Pack'.
~ Added overhauls for a bunch of the vanilla buildings.
~ Re-added 'Nullscape' for vastly improved world generation in The End dimension.
~ Reworked the respawning system to allow for players to get a second chance as well as getting absorption upon respawning.
~ Player spawn locations are now random on servers.
~ New combat system and additional weapons.
~ Added proximity voice chat functionality.
~ Replaced home teleport commands with a potion which is also given to players upon respawning.

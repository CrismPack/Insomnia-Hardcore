v2.2.0

Fabric version: Fabric-0.15.11
Update overview: 
~ Added Quests! (At present it only involves defeating bosses)
~ Added 'Fresh Moves' resource pack, giving some nice animations to the player model.
~ Added 'Daily Rewards' mod, giving players som daily rewards for simply logging in.
~ Added 'Dungeon Now Loading 1%' mod, which includes a new dungeon & boss to the Overworld.
~ Added 'Create Enchantment Industry' mod, which is all about handling XP and enchanting with Create.
~ Added 'Emotecraft' mod, allowing players to use emote animations.
~ New content from 'Mine Cells', including new shields and ranged weapons.
~ Switched to use 'FTB Teams' for creating & managing parties.
~ Changed potions to be stackable up to 16.
~ Fixed hearts turning black when having armor equipped. [https://github.com/RaphiMC/ImmediatelyFast/issues/208]
~ Updated mods to their latest version.
